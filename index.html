About Us

We are a community with a vision to bridge the gap between students and professionals. Our mission is to make students industry ready by helping them achieve professionalism. We actively conduct workshops and interactive sessions to help aid us in fulfilling our mission. We were one of the organizing communities of the first ever Rails Girls Kochi workshop along with Kerala Ruby Community. We are turning a year old and so far, the journey has been sweet.
